B. Pharma

B. Pharma is full time 4 Year Degree Course. For Admission in this program must be 10+2 qualified. After completion this course, student get 100% Job in Top Pharma Companies like - Cipla, Himalaya, Dabur, Glenmark, Sun Pharma, Patanjali etc.

D. Pharma

D. Pharma is a career-oriented diploma course in the field of pharmaceutical sciences. After completion of D.Pharm, successful students can work in hospitals, treatment clinics, health departments, etc. It is an excellent choice to make.
